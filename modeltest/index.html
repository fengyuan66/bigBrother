Hack Club AI + Qwen Vision

Summarize your screen or webcam with one captured frame.

This mini demo uses the Hack Club OpenAI-compatible API with qwen/qwen3-vl-235b-a22b-instruct to inspect a live screen share or webcam feed and explain what is happening.

Your screen stream stays local in the browser. Configure HACKCLUB_API_KEY in .env, then only the captured frame is forwarded to the Hack Club API.
