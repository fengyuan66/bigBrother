Tester notes

Reading guide

Watcher: should update every tick, even when the MPA does nothing.

Threshold badge: should climb toward the MPA trigger threshold only on consecutive watcher positives.

MPA: should stay idle until the threshold is reached, then produce an agenda.

Personality: should turn the MPA agenda into the final spoken line, then attach an ElevenLabs voice clip if configured.

Resources: webcam, screenshare, and browser export should be readable without opening raw files.
